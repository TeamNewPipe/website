Extended Privacy

NewPipe does not use the YouTube API nor the Google Play Services.


This means that we do not share any data (except the video URL) with YouTube or Google. Also, the app does not save any data from you or use services that analyze your usage behavior.

Download media

simply download YouTube videos or audios to your device


With NewPipe you can not only watch YouTube videos, but also download them. The app offers different file formats and resolutions for the audios and videos.
The download center provides you a good overview about your downloads and their progress.
