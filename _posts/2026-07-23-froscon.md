We are once again back at [FrOSCon](https://froscon.org/), but it won't look like it does on the sidebar right now.

<!-- more -->
This year, there is a new (senior) face with us: [@theScrabi](https://github.com/theScrabi) joins [@TheAssassin](https://github.com/TheAssassin) and [@Poolitzer](https://github.com/Poolitzer). And also a new change this year is that we have a dev room instead of a stand like [last year]({% link _posts/2025-08-17-froscon-2025.md %}). We hope this enables us to have a productive environment to progress NewPipe, and talk to intrigued people.

We are able to travel there thanks to the [NewPipe e.V.](https://https://newpipe-ev.de/) covering our travel cost, and it made a donation to FrOSCon which needs [even more financial support this year](https://froscon.org/cfp/cfpartners/) to enable this awesome and free conference.

We hope to see some of you there, come say hello we always love to hear how people use our app \o/. And we will ofc do another travel blog.
