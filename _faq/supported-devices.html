--- categories: - bugs type: info title: "Supported devices" ---

NewPipe supports Android phones and tablets, as well as TVs running Android/Google TV, and cars with Android Auto.

On some devices, it may be beneficial to pick the appropriate UI layout manually. This can be done by changing Settings > Appearance > List view mode, which can be toggled between Auto, List, Grid and Card.

NewPipe explicitly does not support Apple devices, whether iOS, macOS, or anything else. There are already good solutions available for those, such as the app Yattee.

There are also OS-agnostic solutions available: websites such as Invidious and Piped, as well as desktop apps like FreeTube.

A team member of the Android Translation Layer published NewPipe to Flathub.
