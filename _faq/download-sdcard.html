--- categories: - download - bugs type: info title: "Download to SDcard fails" ---

We are aware of a bug that causes a crash when downloading a stream to an external SDcard. We are sorry for this inconvenience and trying our best to fix this issue.

Due to an outdated library (Gigaget) we are planing to refactor the whole downlad handling. This might take a while. We've raised a bounty for implementing certain aspects of the new mechanism. You can take a look at the corresponding ticket on GitHub.
